Atlanta Metro = Primary Demand

99.3% of spend lands in the Atlanta DMA, which matches where the demand actually is. Crews working statewide means anywhere in Georgia is fair game at the right job value — no in-state distance restriction is needed.

State-Line Leakage

$21.22 Crossed Into NC / SC

Advantage+ Expansion delivered a small slice across the Georgia state line. That's the only real geographic boundary worth enforcing — leads outside Georgia can't be serviced regardless of price.

Targeting & Lead Qualification

1. Enforce Georgia-Only Delivery

Set targeting to Georgia state and disable Advantage+ audience expansion so no spend crosses into NC, SC, AL, TN, or FL. Inside GA, any job at the right price is fair game — no radius needed.

2. Qualify by Job Value, Not Distance

Add Lead-Form fields for roof age, ownership status, and repair vs. full replacement. Disqualify on job value (renters, mobile homes, sub-threshold repairs) — not on how far the property sits from Atlanta.

3. Bid Harder Where Demand Is Densest

Keep delivery available statewide, but use bid multipliers to weight spend toward zip codes with high single-family detached homeownership. The Atlanta metro stays the primary demand cluster without excluding profitable jobs elsewhere in GA.
