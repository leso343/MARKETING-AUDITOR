Insight

The "Atlanta DMA" Trap

99.3% of spend is correctly centered on Atlanta. However, the Atlanta DMA covers 50+ counties. Local waste is likely hidden here—drivers shouldn't be traveling 60+ miles for inspections.

DMA Leakage

Out-of-State Reach

The $21.22 spent in the NC/SC border region confirms that "Advantage+ Expansion" is reaching beyond the primary service territory, albeit at a low volume.

Geo-Remediation Strategy

1. Move to Radius-Hard-Stop

Ditch "State" or "DMA" targeting. Use a 30-mile fixed radius around the office to ensure every lead is within a profitable drive-time window.

2. Zip Code Exclusion Logic

Manually blacklist zip codes with high rental density or low-income mobile home parks where roof replacements are statistically improbable.

3. Instant Form "City" Verification

Add a mandatory City/Zip question to the Lead Form. If the city is out of bounds, the lead is disqualified before hitting the CRM.
